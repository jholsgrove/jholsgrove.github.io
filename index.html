About Me

I am a hard working individual based in West Sussex who strives to continually learn. My earlier career has seen me doing Front end web design and development, technical recruitment of Engineering personnel for all Engineering & Scientific disciplines as well as conducting Specialist Mapping roles.


I started my testing career in 2013 using Selenium Webdriver with C# DotNet, Specflow/ Gherkin for developing Behavioural Driven Development automated tests of web applications in paralell; using specification by design. I use a variety of tools including but not limited to Visual Studio, TeamCity, AutoIt, GitHub/ Subversion for source control.


I am currently a Test Engineer at Highlight Service Assurance.
